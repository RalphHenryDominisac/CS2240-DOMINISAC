Who was Alan Turing?

An English mathematician, codebreaker, philosopher, and computer scientist, Alan Mathison Turing is best known for the "Turing Machine" which is his 1936 paper that defines the conceptual framework of a theoretical device which has the ability to manipulate certain symbols according to its rules. He is also widely known for the imitation game called the "Turing Test".
